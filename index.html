Featured Project

Predicting Term Deposit Subscription & Targeting Strategy

SQL · Python · scikit-learn · Bank marketing campaign data (41,188 clients)

CASE STUDY

A Portuguese bank was calling every client in its database to sell term deposits — an 11.3% response rate meant most calls were wasted effort. I built a model to rank customers by likelihood to subscribe, then quantified exactly how much cost that saves versus calling everyone.
